SURF 101

Your go-to guide to catching your first wave!

WHY TAKE UP SURFING?

HEALTH

Get into shape naturally without feeling like you are actually excersising.

TRAVEL

As a surfer sooner or later you'll get tired of surfing the same beach spot and you will feel compelled to discover new beaches and coastlines.

FRIENDS

While waiting for your next wave, you are guaranteed to make new friends by making small talk with fellow surfers as you wait for your next wave.

NATURE

In addition to the new human friends you'll make, you'll also befriend many animals from seals and dolphins to pelicans and much more.
