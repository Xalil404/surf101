FAQs - Frequently Asked Questions

What is the appropriate age to start surfing?

There is no minimum or maximum age to start surfing. If you are capable of walking you are capable of surfing.

What is the minmum budget to start surfing?

Buget around $600 for a surfboard, $200 for a wetsuit, $10 for wax, $20 for a leash and around $30 for a kick pad. So around $850 in total.

Is it a good idea to be friendly with the seals?

Definitely NOT. Stay away from seals as they can be aggressive and most likely attract sharks who will bite you since a seal is faster and can outswim you.

Is it a good idea to be friendly with the Pelicans?

Definitely NOT. Stay away from pelicans; they are jerks!
